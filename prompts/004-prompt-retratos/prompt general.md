# Prompt general - Retratos en blanco y negro


---

## 1) Misma persona, misma pose

### Prompt base
```
Create an ultra-realistic black and white portrait based strictly on the uploaded photo.
Preserve the exact facial structure, proportions, and identity of the person.
Natural masculine features, realistic skin texture with visible pores, freckles and subtle imperfections.
Detailed eyebrows, natural beard and mustache texture, individual hair strands visible.
High-contrast monochrome photography with cinematic chiaroscuro lighting.
Soft shadows defining cheekbones, jawline and nose.
Sharp focus on the eyes, neutral serious expression.
Shallow depth of field, professional studio portrait.
85mm lens look, f/1.8 aesthetic.
Editorial, timeless, dramatic portrait.
Dark or neutral background, no distractions.
Photorealistic, RAW photo quality, extremely detailed.
Do not beautify, do not stylize, do not alter facial features.
```

### Prompt negativo
```
cartoon, anime, illustration, painting, CGI, plastic skin,
over-smoothed face, beauty filter, unrealistic symmetry,
blur, low resolution, noise, artifacts, distorted face,
exaggerated features
```

### Opciones (agrega solo si aplica)
- Rembrandt / cine
```
Rembrandt lighting, deep shadows, cinematic mood.
```
- Arreglar suavizado
```
retain natural skin texture and micro details.
```

## 2) Misma persona, otra pose

### Prompt base
```
Generate a NEW ultra-realistic black and white portrait of the SAME person from the uploaded photo.
Do NOT recreate the same pose, framing or expression.
Create a different portrait with a new pose, angle and composition.
The person should be clearly the same individual, preserving exact facial identity, proportions and unique features.

Ultra-realistic facial details: natural skin texture, pores, freckles, subtle imperfections.
Natural beard and mustache texture, realistic hair strands.
High-contrast monochrome photography.
Cinematic studio lighting with chiaroscuro effect.
Sharp focus on the eyes, shallow depth of field.
Professional editorial portrait, 85mm lens look, f/1.8 aesthetic.
Timeless, dramatic, realistic mood.
Dark or neutral background.

Do not beautify, do not stylize, do not alter facial structure.
Do not copy the original photo composition.
Photorealistic, RAW photo quality.
```

### Prompt negativo
```
same pose, same framing, same expression,
cartoon, anime, illustration, painting, CGI,
plastic skin, beauty filter, over-smoothed face,
distorted proportions, unrealistic symmetry,
blur, low resolution, artifacts
```

### Opciones de pose (elige una)
- Giro leve de rostro
```
three-quarter angle portrait, head slightly turned, eyes looking forward.
```
- Mas editorial
```
head tilted slightly, confident neutral expression.
```
- Mas cinematografico
```
side lighting, face partially in shadow, intense mood.
```
- Mas natural
```
relaxed posture, natural expression, candid portrait style.
```

### Tip muy importante
Si alguna vez ves que "se parece pero no es igual", agrega esta linea:
```
Identity consistency is critical, this must look exactly like the same person.
```
